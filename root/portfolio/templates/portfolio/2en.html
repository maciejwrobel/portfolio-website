Band Finances Manager

The goal of this application is to provide tools that will help music bands in a simple, intuitive way:

- Analyzing recieved offer.
- Order valuation, taking changing conditions into consideration.
- Divide the income, after deducating the costs incurred.

In addition, after logging in, the application allows you to create profiles of individual bands, enter and edit permanent data to further speed up the performance of appropriate calculations.
